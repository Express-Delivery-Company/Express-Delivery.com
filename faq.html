Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on route and service type. Express Air is usually faster than standard shipping.

How do I track my shipment?

Go to the Track Shipment page, enter your Order ID and Email, then view the tracking map and route history.

Can I ship fragile goods?

Yes. Include "fragile" in your description, and our team will advise on packaging requirements.

Is this site connected to live GPS?

Not yet (static version). If client adds more budget, we can integrate real-time tracking with a database and GPS updates.
